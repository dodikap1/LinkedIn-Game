How to play

Threats navigate toward the server. They start moving the moment you place your first firewall — and every 2 seconds they step closer.

Tap any cell to drop a 🔥 firewall. When a threat steps onto a firewall, both are destroyed.

Hit Submit when you're confident your defence will hold — your timer stops immediately and the simulation finishes.

Need help? The 💡 Hint button briefly shows where each threat will step next, but it adds +3 seconds to your time.

When a threat hits a firewall, both are destroyed.

Threat speeds:

⚡Fast — moves 3 cells per second
🦠Standard — moves 2 cells per second
🐌Slow — moves 1 cell per second

You have 8 firewalls to stop them all. Use them wisely — block faster threats first.

Win: Destroy every threat.
Lose: Any threat reaches the server.
